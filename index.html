1. ONMETOISM

Onmetoism/Temnoism is the founding metaphysical belief system of "Ktisism". It might seem unfalsifiable, but it is just designed to be proven by external logic, which becomes its internal logic.

It enforces that metaphysics is the only philosophical branch, as it defines philosophy based on the answer of Nature, and not its question.

Ktisism is a metaphysical belief that Nature creates with the potential of the elements, one in the middle, two below and above.

Onmetoism is a perfectly compatible belief system and philosophy to combine with religions that merely worship one or more deities, but it can also serve as a primary religion.

"Temnō" in the Onmetoist definition is to draw an edge towards division and union, it is reversed in Onmetoism's name due to its triple duality of Nature.

It has three forms: Emotion Onmetoism, Instinct Onmetoism, and Divine Onmetoism; the path of emotion, the path of instinct, and the path of potential, or the cycle of division rather than the spiral of union.

A Temnoist is a recent initiate believer in Onmetoism who is still in pursuit of the conflict of consciousness and awareness. A Temnoist becomes an Onmetoist once he is able to observe the friction of consciousness and awareness.

The text of Onmetoism is strictly intended to be read personally and not to be translated, as every misinterpreted word is harmful; it is not intended to be interpreted in one's own words, but in one's own thoughts; words are often not enough to describe Onmetoism. Every single fragment of the text is purposeful. Those who overshrink and share this text despite these risks are not to be viewed as a representation of Onmetoism, but of Temnoism. When one Onmetoist shall speak of the doctrine of Onmetoism, the second Onmetoist shall not respond of the doctrine of Onmetoism.
