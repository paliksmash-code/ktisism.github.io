ONMETOISM

Seven Truths of Onmetoism:

0. Tomē (Potential)

01 What you see in the middle is either a stranger or yourself.
02 Awareness is a stranger that only knows consciousness; who are you?
03 You will be conscious because you will not know; you will not be aware, because you will understand.
04 It is not truth unless it is aware of, and it is not lie unless it is conscious of.
05 Consciousness is the only judgment of Nature; awareness is the judge.
06 Consciousness is present due to awareness.
07 It reproduces potential.
08 Potential fills the gap between high, middle, and low; from the middle.
09 There is existence because of inexistence, and there is inexistence because of existence.
10 A being is born; its potential allows imagination, its imagination allows intention, its intention allows friction, its friction allows action, its action allows reaction, its reaction allows naught, and naught allows iteration; Nature allows the individual, the individual allows the heart, the heart allows the body, the body allows the throat, the throat allows the mind, the mind allows the will, the will allows decision, and decision allows potential.
